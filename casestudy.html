Overview



About SPARK

This organization ensures that we have access to sustainable resources, housing, employment benefits, culturally humble treatment and care, debt free education, and much more - for ourselves, our families, and our communities. It is the ability to navigate life without fear and barriers that are put in place because of the identities that we hold. SPARK is black, brown, woman, queer, trans, non-binary, indigenous - SPARK is US.



Research



Problem

SPARK was designed to inform and educate women and people in the LGBTQ+ community on their rights and the pressing issues they face. We have observed that the website isn't clear on it's purpose, which is causing these individuals to not have the necessary information they need to move forward.







Solution

We believe that creating a web design that is clear on informing and advocating for women and LGBTQ+ rights will result in a large percentage of people feeling educated about their rights and reproductive justice as it relates to their bodies.
